PEA-SOUP

Description

This is a great New Year's meal! Perfect on a cold winter night. Great comfort food. Serve with corn bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cups water
  • 3 (15 ounce) cans black-eyed peas, drained
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers (such as RO*TEL®), undrained
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • ¾ teaspoon garlic salt
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies
  • 4 teaspoons molasses
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin

    Procedure

  1. Step 1

    In a large Dutch oven, cook with the onion over medium heat , 3-4 minutes; Pour in the water, and stir in black-eyed peas, diced tomatoes, tomatoes with green chiles, Worcestershire sauce, garlic salt, salt, canned green chilies, molasses,black pepper, and cumin until thoroughly mixed.

  2. Step 2

    Bring the soup to a boil, reduce heat to a simmer, cover, and simmer for 45 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    Soup is ready to be served hot.
